# Privacy Policy

Nutrifit is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information.

## Information We Collect

- Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture (if you choose to provide one).
- Health Information: We collect information you provide about your health goals, dietary preferences, and allergies. We also collect information from your meal photos, including the food items you eat and their nutritional content.
- Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the app, such as the features you use, the time you spend in the app, and the actions you take.
- Device Information: We collect information about your device, such as the device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and improve the app: We use your information to provide the app's features, personalize your experience, and improve the app's functionality.
- Provide personalized nutrition guidance: We use your information to provide you with personalized meal plans, nutrition advice, and progress tracking.
- Communicate with you: We may use your information to send you emails about the app, new features, or promotional offers. You can opt out of these emails at any time.
- Protect our users and the app: We may use your information to detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and other harmful activities.

## How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with:

- Service providers: We may share your information with service providers who help us with things like data storage, analytics, and customer support.
- Other users: Your profile picture and name may be visible to other users of the app.
- Aggregated data: We may share aggregated data with third parties for research or marketing purposes. Aggregated data does not identify you personally.

## Your Choices

You can:

- Access and update your information: You can access and update your information in the app settings.
- Delete your account: You can delete your account at any time by contacting us.
- Opt out of marketing emails: You can opt out of marketing emails by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email.

## Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any material changes, we will notify you by email or through the app.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at eliaskassell@gmail.com.
